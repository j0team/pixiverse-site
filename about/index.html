What Pixiverse is

Pixiverse is a gamified productivity app built around a study room, quest-style tasks, timed focus sessions, XP, and multiplayer social features.

It is designed to make daily progress feel a little more playful without hiding the work you actually need to do.

What you can do

  • Create and manage quests
  • Run timed study sessions
  • Earn XP and level up stats
  • Connect with friends in shared rooms

Project stack

  • Godot 4.6 client
  • Nakama backend
  • PostgreSQL storage
